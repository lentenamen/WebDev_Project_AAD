About us


Welcome to Anime Haven, where a team of likeminded individuals came together to bring the community together.


Origins

Anime Haven was created when a group of anime fanatics at ComicCon realised that some of the merch they wanted was just not there. Although the vibrant convention felt so full

and overwhelming, the lack of specific merch left a big hole in their hearts. This led to the eventual birth of Anime Haven.



What You'll Find Here

At Anime Haven, our group strives to achieve satisfaction among anime fans, whether you are a die-hard fan or just a casual watcher we are sure that you will enjoy our website. We will try our hardest to keep up to date with latest trends

as making sure everyone stays in the loop.
