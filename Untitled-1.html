About us


Welcome to Anime Haven, where a team of likeminded individuals came together to bring the community together.


Origins

Anime Haven was created when a group of anime fanatics at ComicCon realised that some of the merch they wanted was just not there. Although the vibrant convention felt so full and overwhelming, the lack of specific merch left a big hole in their hearts. This led to the eventual birth of Anime Haven.
